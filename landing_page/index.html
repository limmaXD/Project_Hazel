About Project Hazel

What is Hazel?

Hazel is a web app, designed as an AI-powered companion tailored to fostering PIE (a combination of positivity, intrigue, and empathy). Through AI-powered, text-driven interactions, it intelligently acknowledges and validates the user's feelings, guides them toward calmer perspectives, and delivers encouragement in moments they need it most.

How It Began

Mental Hazel was born from recognizing the growing challenges of mental health, educational support, and meaningful social connection. We discovered technology could bridge these gaps, transforming the relationship between individuals and their well-being while making support accessible to everyone.

Why Choose Hazel?

Unlike voice assistants, Hazel has a physical presence with expressive AMOLED eyes, gesture recognition, emotion detection, and dynamic RGB lighting. It offers specialized modes for studying, music control, curiosity-driven conversations, and personalized morning briefings.

Technology Stack

We leverage cutting-edge technologies to deliver the best experience. Our system is scalable, secure, and built with modern best practices to ensure optimal performance and reliability.
