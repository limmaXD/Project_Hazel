About Project Aura

What is Aura?

Aura is a cute, gamified, AI-powered AI companion powered by Anthropic's AI. It speaks from a friendly, therapeutic, and empathetic spirit. Aura is a supportive AI companion that learns your preferences through guided conversations. With a personality that adapts, Aura is perfect for engaging and meaningful interactions that grow deeper with every use.
