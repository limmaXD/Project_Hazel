What is Aura?

Aura is a web app, designed as an AI-powered companion tailored to fostering PIE (a combination of positivity, intrigue, and empathy). Through AI-powered, text-driven interactions, it intelligently acknowledges and validates the user's feelings, guides them toward calmer perspectives, and delivers encouragement in moments they need it most.

How It Began

Mental Aura was born from recognizing the growing challenges of mental health, educational support, and meaningful social connection. We discovered technology could bridge these gaps, transforming the relationship between individuals and their well-being while making support accessible to everyone.
